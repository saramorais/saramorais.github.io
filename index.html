About

Welcome to my website! My name is Sara Morais and I'm a Web Developer. I grew up in Brazil and moved to New York City in 2012. I when to General Assembly's coding bootcamp in 2015, and I have been coding ever since.


From mockups to coding to testing, I enjoy the development process from start to finish. My priority is to provide an amazing user experience across all devices. Although most of my experience is on the Front End, I am just as interested in understanding Back End technologies.


When I'm not in front of my computer, you may find me watching TV Shows, walking around the city, checking out street fairs and trying new restaurants.
