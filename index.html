About

Welcome to my website! My name is Sara Morais and my goal is to continuously evolve as a Web Developer. I grew up in Goiania, Brazil and have been living in New York City since 2012. After becoming friends with a few bloggers, I became interested in learning how to code and build websites. That led me to enroll in General Assembly's coding bootcamp in 2015, and I have been coding ever since.

From mockups to coding to testing, I enjoy the development process from start to finish. My priority is to provide an amazing user experience across all devices. Although most of my experience is on the Frontend, I am just as interested in understanding Backend technologies.

When I'm not in front of my computer, you may find me watching TV Shows, or walking around the city, checking out street fairs and trying new restaurants.
